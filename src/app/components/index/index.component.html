Pre becoming owner of the puppy Just wanting to have a dog is not enough. You have to be prepared for a new full-fledged family member with a personal temperament and character. There are many things you need to know before raising a dog, so I recommend being as vigilant and prudent as possible. Hurrying to buy a dog, you will be happy only until problems arise. When you are truly ready to take on this responsibility, you will be happy every moment of your life with your new friend.
Enough time
The more time you spend with your pet, the more he understands.
Hight partience
A dog is not a cat that sleeps quietly in bed, so be prepared for constant cleaning
Readiness for difficulties
Don`t all breeds can boast of high intelligence, so be prepared for difficulties in training.
Fixed costs
Are you ready to constantly spend money on healthy balanced food, vaccinations and vitamins for your pet?
Sufficient care
If the dog will live in the yard - do not worry, but constant combing and bathing for a room dog are required

CARE

We have a duty to take care of our pets, to notice poor health or various diseases

Help

In everything we must help our pets. In no case can our dogs be beaten

TRAVEL

Our pets need constant fun walks. After all, dogs need their children somewhere for their energy. It is best to do this without a leash

FRIEND-SHIP

Being a true friend to the dog and he will protect you in case of danger, of course, if you have both protective breeds, but even small decorative breeds do their best to wink to protect you (as funny as it sounds)

DOG-HOME

We must take the dog into the house as a real new member of the family, he must have a personal place to sleep, but the dog can sleep with you until the molting period.

DOG-FOOD

In order for a dog to grow up healthy and strong, we must feed it with the right rational grooming, adding weight, height and features of the breed.
